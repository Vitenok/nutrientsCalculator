How to use calculator

For every successful diet and a healthy living it is extremly important to not only consume certain amount of calories, but also eat enough macro-nutrients, such as proteins, carbohydrates and fat.

Our calculator will help you calculate your energy need and construct an optimal daily meal plan which will have maximum nutrients needed to support your health without breaking caloric estimate.

In order to calculate optimal menu you will go trough several steps:

    • Input parameters like age, sex, activity level, goal etc.
      Correct calorie intake and macronutrients distribution if you want
      Choose foods and sport supplements you're going to eat
